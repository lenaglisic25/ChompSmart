# Senior Project, ChompSmart

## TLDR
ChompSmart's goal is to support sustainable behavior for individuals mananging chronic conditions. We are trying to make  a platform that will give accurate nutrition guidance to help these individuals make better food decisions. This is with the uses of personalized recipes, nutrution tracking, and communication. We are aiming to help users self-manage their health in an easy and effective way to help them reach their goals!

## Features
### Personal Dashboard
  - Able to give suggestions based on different profile data
    - Demograpgics
    - Health conditions
    - Activity Level
    - Dietary restrictions
    - Cooking skill level
    - Budget and grocery access
      
### Custom Tracking on food intake throughout the day
  - AI food suggestions on meals, based on pantry or fridge contents
  - Photo recognistion of foods
  - Videos/Recepies on Dishes to help people hit their recipies
  - Charts and summaries of trends

### Communication Engine
  - Personalized AI notifications to encource the usaged, and to keep track of goals
  - Reminders to input data/document
  - Communication about grocery reminders
  - Weekly Summary and Support
